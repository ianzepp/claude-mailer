## IMPORTANT NOTES FOR FOREIGN BUYERS

1. **Legal Considerations:**
   - Foreigners can legally purchase real estate in Colombia with few restrictions
   - Properties near borders or coastlines may have additional regulations
   - Strongly recommended to work with a bilingual lawyer (abogado)
   - Title verification (certificado de tradición) is critical

2. **Due Diligence:**
   - Verify property titles through Oficina de Registro
   - Check for liens, encumbrances, or legal issues
   - Rural properties: verify water rights, access roads, utilities
   - Environmental permits for construction/renovation

3. **Transaction Process:**
   - Typically requires Colombian bank account
   - Notary (notaría) required for official transaction
   - Property taxes and transaction costs vary by municipality
   - Consider working with a gestor for paperwork

4. **Language:**
   - Many agencies in Bogotá have English-speaking agents
   - Rural areas less likely to have English services
   - Bilingual lawyer highly recommended
   - Consider hiring a translator for viewings and negotiations
